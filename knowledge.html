Common questions, honest answers

Questions families ask.

How much life insurance do I actually need?
A simple rule of thumb: 10–15 times your annual income, plus any outstanding loans. So if you earn ₹6 lakh a year and have a ₹20 lakh home loan, aim for roughly ₹80 lakh–₹1 crore of cover. A pure term plan makes this affordable. We'll help you calculate your exact number based on your family's needs.
Is LIC still worth it when so many private insurers exist?
LIC remains India's largest life insurer, backed by the Government of India, with the largest claim-settlement track record in the industry. Private insurers are also regulated and legitimate — the real question is not the company alone but whether the plan fits your need and whether someone will stand with your family at claim time. That service is what we provide.
What tax benefits do LIC policies give?
Premiums generally qualify for deduction under Section 80C (up to ₹1.5 lakh per year, old tax regime), and maturity proceeds may be tax-free under Section 10(10D), subject to conditions on the premium-to-sum-assured ratio and current tax rules. Tax laws change — we'll explain what applies to your specific policy before you buy.
My old LIC policy has lapsed. Can I revive it?
Usually yes. LIC allows revival of lapsed policies within a specified period (typically 5 years from the first unpaid premium) by paying pending premiums with interest, sometimes with a health declaration. LIC also announces special revival campaigns with concessions. Bring us your policy number — we'll check the status and handle the process for you.
What happens if I stop paying premiums?
If you've paid premiums for a minimum period (as per your plan), the policy acquires a paid-up value — cover continues at a reduced level. You may also surrender it for its surrender value, though this usually means a loss. Before you stop paying, talk to us — options like a policy loan or premium-mode change often solve the cash-flow problem without losing the policy.
How do claims work? Will my family struggle to get the money?
This is exactly why buying through a local agent matters. At claim time, we personally help the family collect documents, file the claim, and follow up with the branch until settlement. Most genuine claims with complete documents are settled within days to a few weeks. Your family will never have to navigate it alone.
